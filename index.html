How to use:

Copy the list you want to modify from the "CS Internship - Queue" group and paste it into the Input section. Click on Submit button, and in the Edit List section, make the desired changes to the list. You can move someone to the end of the list, remove them, or add a new member. Once you've made the desired changes, click the copy button in the Output section, and the updated list will be copied for you.
