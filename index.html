How to use the app:

Copy the list you wish to modify from the "CS Internship - Queue" group and paste it into the Input section. After clicking the Submit button, you can make the necessary changes to the list in the Edit List section. These changes may involve moving someone to the end of the queue, removing them, or adding a new member. Once you've completed the desired changes, click the copy button in the Output section to have the updated list copied to your clipboard.
