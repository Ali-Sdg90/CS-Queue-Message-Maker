How to Use the App:

To modify a list in the "CS Internship - Queue" group, start by navigating to the group and copying the list you want to modify. Next, go to the "Input List" section of the app, paste the copied list, and click the "Submit" button. This action will populate the "Edit List" section with your list data.
In the "Edit List" section, you can make the necessary changes to the list. You have the options to move members to the end of the list, remove members, or add new members.
Once you have completed the desired changes, go to the "Output List" section and click the "Copy" button to copy the updated list to your clipboard. Finally, paste the updated list back into the "CS Internship - Queue" group.
